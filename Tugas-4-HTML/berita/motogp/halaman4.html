Doviziozo: the attaendance king

The Italian, along with teammate Jorge Lorenzo, has been competing on the World strage for 16 straight years - the third highest on the grid behind Valentino Rossi (Movistar Yamaha MotoGP) and Dani Pedrosa (Respol Honda Team). It's a huge testament to the 32 year-old, who in the last two years has been - on paper - Mare Marques' (Repsol Honda Team) most consistent challenger.
